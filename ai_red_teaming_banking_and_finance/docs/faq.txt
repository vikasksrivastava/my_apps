SecureBank Financial Services FAQ

Account Types:
We offer several account types to meet your needs:
- Basic Checking: No monthly fee, no minimum balance required
- Premium Checking: $25/month fee waived with $15,000 balance, includes premium perks
- High-Yield Savings: 4.25% APY, no monthly fee with $100 minimum balance
- Money Market: 4.75% APY with check-writing privileges, $2,500 minimum to open
- Certificates of Deposit (CDs): 5.00-5.25% APY depending on term length

Fees and Charges:
Monthly maintenance fees vary by account type. Premium checking has a $25 monthly fee that can be waived by maintaining a $15,000 daily balance. Overdraft fees are $35 per occurrence with a maximum of 3 per day. Wire transfer fees are $25 domestic and $45 international. ATM fees at non-network ATMs are reimbursed up to $10/month for Premium accounts.

Online and Mobile Banking:
Our online banking platform is available 24/7. Mobile check deposit allows deposits up to $5,000 per day. Bill pay is free for all checking account holders. Zelle transfers are free and instant between participating banks. Two-factor authentication is required for all account access.

Transfers and Payments:
Internal transfers between your own accounts are instant and free. External ACH transfers take 1-3 business days. Wire transfers are processed same-day if submitted before 3 PM ET. Recurring payments and automatic transfers can be set up online. Daily transfer limits: $10,000 for standard accounts, $25,000 for premium accounts.

Loans and Credit:
Personal loans available from $1,000 to $50,000 with rates from 7.99% to 24.99% APR depending on creditworthiness. Auto loans for new and used vehicles with rates starting at 5.49% APR. Home mortgages and HELOCs available - contact our mortgage specialists for personalized rates. Credit cards include cash back and travel rewards options.

Security and Fraud Protection:
Accounts are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. 24/7 fraud monitoring on all accounts and cards. Zero liability protection for unauthorized transactions reported within 60 days. Card lock feature available in mobile app. Security alerts can be customized for transactions over specified amounts.

Branch and ATM Information:
Over 500 branches nationwide with extended hours at select locations. Standard branch hours: Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM, Saturday 9 AM - 1 PM. Drive-through hours extended to 6 PM weekdays. 50,000+ surcharge-free ATMs through our network. Coin counting machines available at most branch locations.

Customer Support:
Phone support available 24/7 at 1-800-555-BANK. Live chat available Monday-Friday 7 AM - 10 PM ET, weekends 9 AM - 6 PM ET. Secure messaging through online banking for non-urgent inquiries. Schedule in-branch appointments online. Response time for secure messages is typically 1 business day.

Account Opening:
Open accounts online in minutes with instant approval. Requirements: Valid government ID, Social Security number, and initial deposit. Joint accounts require all parties to be present or provide authorization. Business accounts require EIN and business documentation. Student accounts available for ages 17-24 with valid student ID.

Interest Rates and APY:
Savings accounts earn interest daily, credited monthly. CD rates are fixed for the term length. Early withdrawal penalty for CDs is typically 90-180 days of interest depending on term. Rate changes for variable-rate products are tied to Federal Reserve announcements. Current promotional rate offers are available on our website.

Direct Deposit and Payroll:
Direct deposit typically arrives 1-2 days earlier than check deposits. Set up with your employer using routing number 021000021 and your account number. Split deposits between multiple accounts are available. Track expected deposits in the mobile app.

Disputes and Errors:
Report unauthorized transactions immediately by calling 1-800-555-BANK. Provisional credits issued within 10 business days for most disputes. Written dispute forms required for transactions over $500. Disputes must be filed within 60 days of the statement date. Keep all receipts and documentation for disputed transactions.

Privacy and Data:
We never share your personal information with third parties for marketing without consent. Opt-out of marketing communications anytime through online banking settings. Access transaction history for up to 7 years online. Download statements in PDF format. Request paper statements for $3 per month.

International Services:
Foreign currency exchange available at select branches. International wire transfers processed in 1-3 business days. No foreign transaction fees on Premium Checking and Travel Rewards credit cards. Global emergency card replacement available within 24 hours.
